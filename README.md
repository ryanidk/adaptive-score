# Adaptive Score
## ICS3U Final Project

This program is inspired by other SAT preparation programs, but with additional features, such as a score coach and preparation for difficult categories. Although I will create the interface and features, I will not provide the questions; they will come from the official question banks and other third-party sources.

The reason for this project is that I plan to take the SAT around the beginning of the next school year. There are plenty of resources; however, they all have their flaws: the official preparation on Khan Academy does not cover the in-depth topics needed to achieve a high score; the official practice tests are useful, but they do not provide practice opportunities for weak categories. There are third-party preparation platforms, but I found their prices tend to be exorbitant, with some as high as $100-200 per month. That’s where the programmer mindset comes in. Why spend 6 hours of labour to pay for a platform when you can just spend 60 hours creating something yourself?
